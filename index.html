Personal Background

Ambassador Ibikunle Olawale Waliyy is a distinguished entrepreneur, renowned business mogul, and a celebrated Phoenix Ambassador of Fohow International. Born in the 1970s in Kwara State, Nigeria, he hails from the esteemed family of late Alhaja and Alhaji Ibikunle. His journey is one of resilience, excellence, and unparalleled dedication, leaving a profound impact on the health, wellness, and entrepreneurial sectors worldwide.

Education

From humble beginnings at Ago-Owu Primary School in Shogunle, Lagos, to Oshodi High School, Mr. Ibikunle's academic foundation laid the groundwork for his future successes. He later earned a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from the prestigious University of Ilorin, marking his dedication to academic excellence and professional growth.

Career and Achievements

Throughout his career, Mr. Ibikunle has combined his engineering background with a passion for alternative medicine, becoming a certified Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) practitioner through the Fohow International Business School. He is also certified by the Lagos State Traditional Medicine Board. As a result, he has not only excelled in entrepreneurship but also in promoting holistic wellness, empowering thousands of individuals worldwide.

Key Areas of Expertise

Qualifications of a Distinguished Entrepreneur

Ambassador Ibikunle Olawale Waliyy’s qualifications span several key areas of expertise:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering: University of Ilorin, Nigeria.
  • Certificate in Information Technology: Demonstrating his commitment to staying at the forefront of technological advancements.
  • Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Certification: Obtained through Fohow International Business School, showcasing his dedication to alternative medicine and holistic health.
  • Lagos State Traditional Medicine Board Certification: Official recognition of his TCM practice in Nigeria.

These qualifications have enabled him to provide a unique blend of engineering precision and holistic wellness to those he mentors and advises.

On the Mission

Business Tour and Strategies

A visionary in entrepreneurship and business strategies, Ambassador Ibikunle Olawale Waliyy has toured the world, promoting wellness and wealth creation through strategic partnerships. Since joining Fohow International in 2012, his rise to success has been nothing short of meteoric. By April 2013, he achieved the status of Saphire partner, followed by his elevation to one-diamond director in October of the same year, and later achieving three-diamond director by early 2015.

His international business tours have taken him to Moscow, China, Dubai, and other key global destinations, where he has led seminars and workshops to empower entrepreneurs in the health and wellness space. His success is built on strategic thinking, innovative marketing, and a relentless drive to foster empowerment through education and holistic well-being.

Championing Growth and Opportunity

Empowering Communities and Transforming Lives

Ambassador Ibikunle Olawale Waliyy is a passionate advocate for empowerment, focusing on equipping individuals with the tools needed to achieve financial freedom and holistic wellness. Through his role as a business consultant and success coach, he has impacted thousands of lives, mentoring emerging entrepreneurs and professionals in health and network marketing.

Notably, he became the first five-diamond director of Fohow International in Nigeria in 2018, and by March 2020, he had reached the esteemed status of the first seven-diamond director in the country. His story continues to inspire as he leads empowerment programs focused on health, wealth creation, and personal development.
